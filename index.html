Sustainable Rabbit Farming

Practical, ethical rabbit farming focused on food security, income generation, and quality breeding in Namibia.

About Rabbitman

Rabbitman Farms is an emerging rabbit farming initiative aimed at producing healthy rabbits for meat, breeding, and future farmer support. The project focuses on low-cost, sustainable, and scalable farming methods.

Why Rabbit Farming?

Rabbits produce lean, nutritious meat with high protein content.

They require minimal space and can be farmed efficiently.

Fast growth and reproduction make rabbits ideal for small-scale farming.

Our Focus Breeds

New Zealand White

Popular meat breed known for fast growth and good feed conversion.

Californian

Hardy breed suitable for warm climates and meat production.

Local Crossbreeds

Adapted to local conditions and suitable for smallholder systems.
