About Rabbitman

Rabbitman is a small-scale rabbit farming initiative focused on sustainable meat production, breeding, and responsible animal care. Our aim is to contribute to food security, income generation, and practical farming solutions.

Why Rabbit Farming?

  • High-quality, lean and nutritious meat
  • Low space and feed requirements
  • Fast reproduction and growth rates
  • Suitable for small-scale and urban farming

Products & Services

  • Breeding Rabbits
  • Rabbit Meat (on order)
  • Manure for Organic Fertiliser
  • Training & Starter Guidance (future)

Future Plans

The farm intends to expand into structured breeding programs, value-added products, and farmer training to support rabbit farming adoption in Namibia.
