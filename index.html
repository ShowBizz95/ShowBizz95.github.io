About Rabbitman

Rabbitman is a small-scale rabbit farming initiative based in Windhoek, Namibia. The farm focuses on sustainable meat production, breeding, and responsible animal care using practical, low-cost methods suitable for local conditions.

Why Rabbit Farming?

Lean, nutritious meat with high protein content.

Low space and feed requirements.

Fast reproduction and quick returns for small farmers.
